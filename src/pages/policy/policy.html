Privacy Policy

Welcome to The Banished! Your privacy matters to us.

Our Privacy Policy, accessible at Banished , outlines how we handle your information. This document explains what data we collect and how we use it at Black Fox LLC.

Feel free to reach out if you have questions or need more details about our Privacy Policy. We're here to help. Your Privacy Matters

At The Banished, we care about your privacy. When you use our website, you're giving your consent to our Privacy Policy and agreeing to its terms.

Your Data and How We Use It

We want you to know that the information we collect is important, and we're transparent about why we need it.

If you reach out to us, we might gather details like your name, email, phone number, and what you've told us. And when you sign up for an Account, we'll ask for your contact info – things like your name, company name, address, email, and phone number.

We'll always let you know why we're asking for your personal info when we do.

How We Use Your Information

We want you to know how we handle the info you share with us. Here's what we do with it:


  • Make our website work and keep it running smoothly.
  • Make our website better and more suited to you.
  • Learn how you use our website.
  • Come up with new stuff you might like.
  • Talk to you – for support, updates, and cool things we think you'll want to know about.
  • Send you emails when needed.
  • Keep an eye out for anything fishy.

We're here to make your experience great.

Log Files

We keep track of some basic stuff when you visit The Banished. This includes things like your IP address, the type of browser you're using, when you came to our site, and what pages you looked at. Don't worry, this info doesn't point back to you personally. We use it to see what's popular, fix any issues, and understand how people use our site. It helps us keep things running smoothly for you!

Cookies and Web Beacons

Just like most websites, we use 'cookies' to make things smoother for you. These cookies store info about what you like and the pages you visit. This helps us make your experience better by tailoring our content to what you enjoy.

Cookies also tell us your browser type and other basic details to improve our site. For more info on cookies in general, check out "What Are Cookies" by Cookie Consent.

GDPR Data Protection Rights

We want you to know your data rights. You can:


  • Ask for a copy of your info.
  • Fix info you think is wrong or incomplete.
  • Request we delete your data in some cases.
  • Ask us to limit how we use your data in some cases.
  • Object to how we use your data in some cases.
  • Move your data to another place or to you, in some cases.

To use these rights, just contact us. Your data, your control!

Caring for Kids' Privacy

We care about kids' safety online. Parents, please watch over your kids and join in their online journey.

The Banished doesn't knowingly gather any personal info from kids under 13. If you think your child shared info with us, let us know. We'll work fast to remove it. Your child's safety matters!
